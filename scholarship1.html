National Merit Scholarship

Description: Scholarship for students with excellent academic performance after SSLC/10th standard.

Eligibility:

  • Indian citizen
  • Passed SSLC/Class 10 with high marks
  • Enrolled in PUC

Benefits:

  • Tuition fee support
  • Maintenance allowance via NSP portal

Official Link: National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
