Karnataka State Scholarship

Description: State-level scholarships provided through SSP for various categories such as SC, ST, OBC, EWS, and Minority students.

Eligibility:

  • Resident of Karnataka
  • Belong to an eligible category

Benefits:

  • Tuition fee reimbursement
  • Hostel and maintenance allowance
