Post Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC)

Description: Karnataka government scholarship for students belonging to SC, ST, and OBC categories to support post-matric education.

Eligibility:

  • Resident of Karnataka
  • Belongs to SC / ST / OBC category
  • Enrolled in PUC or higher education

Benefits:

  • Full or partial tuition fee reimbursement
  • Maintenance and hostel allowance
