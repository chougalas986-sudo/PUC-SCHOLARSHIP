Minority Scholarship

Description: Scholarship for students belonging to minority communities such as Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Jain, Buddhist, and Parsi in Karnataka.

Eligibility:

  • Belongs to a recognized minority community
  • Annual family income ≤ ₹2,00,000
  • Enrolled in PUC or equivalent course

Benefits:

  • Maintenance allowance
  • Tuition fee support through NSP

Official Link: National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
